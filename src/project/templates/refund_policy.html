Refund Policy

Last updated June 25, 2026

This Refund Policy explains how refunds work for paid plans purchased from Globalify ("Globalify," "we," "us," or "our") through our website at https://globalify.org. This policy forms part of our Terms of Service.

Merchant of Record

Our order process and payments are conducted by our online reseller and Merchant of Record, Paddle.com Market Limited and its affiliates ("Paddle"). Paddle processes payments, issues invoices, and handles refunds on our behalf. Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method used at checkout. Your purchase is also subject to Paddle's Buyer Terms and Conditions.

14-day money-back guarantee

We want you to be satisfied with Globalify Pro. If you are not happy with your purchase, you may request a full refund within fourteen (14) days of the original purchase date. This applies to both subscription and one-time ("lifetime") purchases.

Subscription renewals

Subscriptions renew automatically at the end of each billing period. You can cancel at any time from your account settings or by contacting us, and cancellation will stop all future renewals. If you were charged for a renewal that you did not intend, contact us within fourteen (14) days of that renewal charge and we will work with you in good faith to resolve it, which may include a refund of the most recent renewal.

To avoid being charged for the next billing period, please cancel before your renewal date. Once a new billing period begins, your access continues for that period.

How to request a refund

To request a refund, please contact us with the details of your purchase:

  • Email info@globalify.org from the address associated with your account; and
  • Include the order or receipt number from your Paddle confirmation email (if available).

We aim to review and respond to refund requests within a few business days. Once a refund is approved and processed by Paddle, it may take several additional business days for the funds to appear on your statement, depending on your bank or card issuer.

Exceptions

We may decline a refund request where there is evidence of fraud, abuse of this policy, or use of the Services in violation of our Terms of Service (for example, automated scraping or bulk extraction of data). Where required by applicable consumer protection law, you may have additional refund or cancellation rights that this policy does not limit.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this Refund Policy, please contact us at info@globalify.org.
