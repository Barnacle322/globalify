Frequently asked questions

What's included in contact details?
Pro subscribers can view email addresses and phone numbers for investors and firms that have made them available in the database. Free users see only public social links and websites.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled at any time with no questions asked. Lifetime access is a one-time purchase with no recurring charges.
Is there a free trial?
The Free tier gives you unlimited search and browsing so you can evaluate the database before upgrading. No credit card required.
What does a Pro subscription give me access to?
Pro unlocks full contact details, advanced search filters, unlimited bookmarks, and CSV export of database records. It is a software subscription to a database tool, not a regulated financial service.
